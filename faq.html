There are three ways to order!

Ordering Online

To order online:
1. Select your items from the Products page and add them to your cart.
2. Go to cart and pay for selected items. You must pay using credit or debit.

Ordering In-Person

We'd love to see you! All orders can be placed in person at our bakery shop located at Humber college. Visit the Contact Us page for our location.

Ordering via Telephone

You can give us a call to place an order in advance. Please see our Contact Us page for our phone number.
